- Introduction

Product Development AI Engineer,
based in Nanking, China.

NLP, LLMs, Data Science, ITS(intelligent transportation system)

Hi, I am Zecheng Chow.

Currently, I work at Jiangsu Hoski Network Technology Co., Ltd., where I also serve as a co-founder.

I obtained my master's degree in Data Science from the University of Sydney,and the Capstone was under the guidance of Dr. Ali Braytee from the University of Technology Sydney. Before joining my current company, I earned a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Huaiyin Institute of Technology, under the supervision of Professor Shangbing Gao.

Initially, I worked as a software engineer, covering various products and programming languages. Following my master's graduation, my research interests expanded to encompass diverse topics such as multimodal language models, autonomous driving, end-to-end vehicle planning, high-definition mapping, GIS imitation learning, and more. My focus lies in NLP and LLMs to empower the intelligent transportation domain where I work, with the aspiration to apply these technologies in meteorology and smart city design in the future. The primary challenge lies in enabling artificial intelligence to accurately comprehend its surrounding environment, devise reasonable plans, and make informed decisions accordingly.

Drop me an email if you are interested in these topics. Collaborations in any form are welcomed!
